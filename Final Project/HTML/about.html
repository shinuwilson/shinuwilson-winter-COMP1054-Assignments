Our Story

Founded in 1984, Fossil has always been inspired by American creativity and ingenuity. We bring new life into the watch industry by making quality, fashionable watches that are both fun and accessible.

Our team is dedicated to pushing the boundaries of style and innovation while staying true to the timeless appeal of vintage aesthetics.

At Fossil, we believe that great design starts with great stories. And every watch we make tells one.
